Business Card Video Playback

Key Functionality

  • Play video on business card for devices that support (devices that don't support will play full screen)
  • Optionally play video full screen in native player

Targets

Goodcode Business Card

Instructions

  • Point camera at business card to view
  • Tap video to play
  • Double tap to access options menu
